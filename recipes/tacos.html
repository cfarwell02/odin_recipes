Tacos

Description

Ingredients

  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Salsa
  • Onion

Instructions

  1. Chop the onion.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water (about 1/4 to 1/2 cup). Stir and let simmer for a few minutes until well mixed and thickened.
  5. For corn or flour tortillas, warm them in a dry skillet for about 30 seconds per side, or wrap them in a damp paper towel and microwave for about 30 seconds.
  6. Lay out a tortilla.
  7. Spoon in some seasoned ground beef.
  8. Add shredded lettuce and cheese.
  9. Top with salsa and a dollop of sour cream.

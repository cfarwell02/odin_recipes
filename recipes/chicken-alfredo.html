Chicken Alfredo

Description

Pasta covered in an ultra-rich and creamy sauce and topped with juicy strips of seared chicken.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Olive oil/butter
  • Seasonings of your choice
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pasta of your choice

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil or butter over medium heat.
  3. Season the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Remove the chicken and let it rest before slicing.
  5. In the same skillet, add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  6. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly.
  8. Serve topped with sliced chicken and additional Parmesan if desired.
